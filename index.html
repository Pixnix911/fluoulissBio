How to install?

  1. Open your text file
  2. Copy your script
  3. Open tampermonkey.com and install the extension
  4. Go to sakura.mba website
  5. Click on the Tampermonkey icon in your browser toolbar
  6. Select "Add new script"
  7. Paste your script into the "New Script" editor
  8. Save and enjoy!
